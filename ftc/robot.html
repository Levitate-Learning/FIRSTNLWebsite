2025 FTC Starter Bot

The REV Robotics starter bot is a kit designed to be built if you have the EDU Kit V2 to Starter Kit V3.1 Upgrade or the FTC Starter Bot V3.1. We do not provide all the materials to build this robot, your team may fundraise to buy the components missing to create this build. Check out the section below for some recommended optional kits.

The provided EduKit V2 robot does not contain all the parts required for this build guide. You will need to fundraise for additional components to complete this build.
REV Robotics Canada

Optional Robot Expansion

We provide to new teams the REV Edu Kit V2. This kit will allow your team to build a robot and compete in the FTC competition. Teams may fundraise money to purchase additional components for your robot. Fundraising and budget is a component of the engineering portfolio. We recommend teams fundraise money to expand the capabilities of their robot and for team spirit through materials like team shirts.
